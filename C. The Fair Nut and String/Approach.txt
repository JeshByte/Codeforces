For every a, we will calculate the number of valid sequences ending with that particular a. Lets define this as it's contribution. Thus it's contribution is equal to sum of contributions of all as preceding the latest b till now plus 1. So after calculating the current a's contribution, we would store and maintain a running prefix sum of all the contributions till now for computation of the contributions of all the as after it. The sum of all of these contributions is our final answer.
