As frequency matters here, our first intuition is to divide the numbers into buckets based on their frequency. So we will obviously start with the highest frequency and take all instances of an element from that bucket. Lets say the frequency of this element is f. Now I can take at most f-1 instances of the next element I choose. So if the next element has a frequency >=(f-1), I will take f-1 such elements and then I will try to look for f-2 number of elements of another kind and so on. Now lets say I get an element whose frequency is less than the one I am looking for currently. Then I will take all instances of that element and the next frequency I will look for will be 1 less than that of the element I just took. So in this way we will greedily pick our elements and create our final answer.
