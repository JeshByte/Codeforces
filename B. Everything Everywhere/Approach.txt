Lets say the gcd of a subarray is g. Then both the max and min would be multiples of g. But their difference needs to be g too. Thus max=g*(x+1) and min=g*x. But the elements in between them(if any), also need to be multiples of g. But if we try to add any more multiples of g, then our max/min ends up changing. Thus this condition can only be satisfied in subarrays of length 2. So just check for the 2 length subarrays and count accordingly to get the final answer.
