Elements that are themselves divisible by 6, would contribute constant number of subarrays irrespective of their position. Now the only other type of valid subarrays are the ones which have an element divisible by 2 and an element divisible by 3. We must try to reduce these type of subarrays. One way of doing this is to arrange the array in this way -> multiples of 6,multiples of 2,extras,multiples of 3.
