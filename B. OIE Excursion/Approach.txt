You will face a problem with crossing guards if they have equal values and all of them are currently at m-1. You could wait it out till they become 0 and then try to cross them. But still you will have to cross all the equal guards before they again turn to 0. You could only do this if the segment of equal values is of length <=m. Thus we just need to find the longest such segment and check whether it's length is <=m.
