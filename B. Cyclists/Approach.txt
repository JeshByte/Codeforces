If the winning card is within the first k cards, it will be always optimal to take that one immediately as choosing any other card is of no use. But if it is not present then we should choose the one with the minimum cost to bring a new card within the first k ones in hope of maybe getting the winning card. This is basically the optimal strategy to get our answer. We can just simulate it using deque to represent the cards being pushed around and multiset to maitain the card of minimum value among the first k ones. This will be within constraints as our code will run as many times we perform the operation. And as per the constraints the number of operations will never go beyond 5000.
