So basically we need to reduce the gaps as much as possible. And we don't need to treat k1 and k2 seperately as they both would be used for reducing these gaps. So we will create a new array with the gaps and lets say we have op=k1+k2 operations to perform. Now with every operation we turn the gap of x to x-1. So their contribution gets reduced by x^2-(x-1)^2=2*x-1. So every time we would want to go for the highest possible x. Thus we can use a max heap and just reduce the highest possible gap till we run out of operations. Then the sum of the squares of the remaining gaps will be our optimal answer.
