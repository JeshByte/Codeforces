z has to be a multiple of x, like y. So both z and y are of the form q*x. So to satisfy the given condition, z's q must not divide y's q. It also needs to be less than y's q as z should be less than y. So whatever is y's q, we can set z's q to one less than it. Then it would never divide y. The only case it won't work is if y's q is 2. Other than that the answer will always be YES.
