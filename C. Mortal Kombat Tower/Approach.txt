We have to use standard 2d dp here. The states and transitions are - 
- dp[i][turn] = The least number of skip points required to kill all the remaining enemies starting from the ith one as per the player's turn(1->friend and 0->you)
- dp[i][0]=min(dp[i+1][1],dp[i+2][1])
- dp[i][1]=min(skips+dp[i+1][0],skips+dp[i+2][0]) where skips will depend on the values of arr[i] and arr[i+1]
- dp[n][turn]=0 -> base condition
- dp[0][1] -> final subproblem
