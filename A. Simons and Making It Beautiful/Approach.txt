Okay so lets say we have an index i which is dirty. Swapping it with an element to its left is of no use because the max element in its prefix remains same. Swapping it with an element to its right won't have any effect on the dirtyness of the other index as the max element in its prefix also remains same. So as we have one operation available, we can just fix any one dirty index at most. So just take the left most dirty index(as it gives us the most options), then swap it with the max element to its right if its greater than the dirty index. This will be our best case answer.
