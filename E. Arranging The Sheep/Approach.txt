Basically we need to compress the sheeps from both sides towards a common middle point. But choosing this middle point will decide the optimality of our solution. So for each possible middle point(basically all the points which have a sheep), the moves needed to compress the sheeps towards it is moves[i]=movesLeft[i]+movesRight[i]. So we can calculate the movesLeft[i] like this -> movesLeft[i-1]+(distance between ith and i-1th sheep)*(number of sheeps before the ith one which is basically i if you use 0 based indexing). The base case here is movesLeft[0]=0. We can find movesRight[i] similarly. Then we need to just go through all these points again, calculate their answer(moves[i]) and the minimum out of them will be our final result.
